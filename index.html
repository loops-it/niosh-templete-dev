About NIOSH

Occupational Safety,
Health & Welfare for all

NIOSH as a trusted OSH Partner always provide solutions for safety and health issues existing in your industry. The goal of the Institute is to provide a world class Occupational Safety and Health (OSH) service to the industry while being a self-financing body. NIOSH is the leader in delivering professional Safety and Health information and services to all our stakeholders.

Way Forward

Future of National Occupational Safety and Health in Sri Lanka

NIOSH is introducing a new Portal to support the industry with AI-powered support bot, a comprehensive Learning Management System (LMS) and a Membership Portal fostering OSH professional’s engagement and access to exclusive OSH resources

Segments

Revolutionizing Workplace Safety through Smart Learning

Membership portal

The Membership Portal connects OSH professionals, offering access to exclusive resources, networking opportunities, industry updates, and tools to enhance their expertise and promote workplace safety excellence.

LMS

It gives opportunities to learn and earn exclusive knowledge and practical application which leads to a CERTIFICATE which supports your future professional development. This will be a game based learning followed by subject specific new updates on OSH.

AI Chat Bot

The AI Chat bot provides instant OSH knowledge, guidance on safety practices, and seamless support for incident reporting, ensuring efficient workplace safety management and compliance. It will give you opportunities to find solutions for your immediate OSH issues.
